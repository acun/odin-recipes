Company Tea

Description

Company tea is a beverage created by mixing sweet labrador tea, Nuka-Cola, cream, and whiskey. Consuming it gives a high boost to AP regeneration and Disease Resistance, restores a small amount of HP, and satisifies a large amount of thirst at a cost of a small amount of rads.

Ingredients

  • Cream (1)
  • Nuka-Cola (1)
  • Sweet Labrador Tea (1)
  • Whiskey (1)

Crafting

  1. Learn Method: Learned from plan/recipe Recipe: Delbert's company tea
  2. Build at: Cooking station
