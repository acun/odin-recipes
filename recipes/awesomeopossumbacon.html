Awesome Opossum Bacon

Description

Opossum meat that has been cooked with wood. Consuming it will give a moderate boost to Luck, satisfy a moderate amount of hunger, and restore a large amount of HP at the cost of moderate rads.

Ingredients

  • Opossum meat (1)
  • Wood (1)

Crafting

  1. Learn Method: Learned on pickup Opossum meat
  2. Build at: Cooking station
