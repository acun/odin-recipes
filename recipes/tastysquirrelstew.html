Tasty Squirrel Stew

Description:

A tasty stew made of squirrel bits, tatos, and boiled water with some added salt and cooked with wood. It greatly reduces hunger and satisfies a moderate amount of thirst while giving a large bonus to XP earned.

Ingredients:

  • Boiled water (2)
  • Salt (1)
  • Squirrel bits (2)
  • Tato (2)
  • Wood (1)

Crafting:

  1. Learn Method: Learned from plan/recipe Recipe: Tasty squirrel stew
  2. Build at: Cooking station
